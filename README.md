# La odisea de Odiseo

A hand-drawn doodle platformer set in the Sonoran Desert. Play as a wandering
tree-planter: swap invasive palms for native Sonoran trees (Palo Verde,
Mezquite, Palo Fierro, Mimbre, Torote, Uña de Gato, Palo Dulce, Guayacán),
dodge the police, stay hydrated, and grow little oases across an endless,
self-generating city on the edge of the desert.

Fully client-side — no build step, no server. Just open `index.html`.

## Controls

**Mobile:** hold left/right to walk · swipe up anywhere to jump · tap the bottom
to replant a palm.
**Keyboard:** ←/→ or A/D to move · Space/↑/W to jump · ↓/S by a palm to replant.

Tap the corner buttons for the tree album (collection) and the sound toggle.
